# Refund & Replacement Guidelines (Support Team Version)

### Key Timeframes
- **Return Window:** Within 30 days from delivery date  
- **Defect/Damage Report:** Must be reported within 7 days of delivery  
- **Processing Time:** Once approved, refund/replacement must be processed within 3 business days

---

## Step 1: Check Customer Eligibility

Retrieve information about order/items the customer have issues with.
Check the delivery date

1. Send request to order platform to retrieve information about order/items the customer have issues with.
2. Answer These Questions:
- Is the request within 30 days of delivery?  
- Was the defect or issue reported within 7 days of delivery?  

If **NO**, go to **Exceptions Policy** (see below).
If **YES** to all, proceed:
1. Identify the Type of Issue
2. Call `check_attachments` to get report by photo/video from customer.
- If attachments doesn't confirm claim, politely refuse user. 
- If attachments confirms the reported problem, offer options to user, and got to Step 3: Process Workflow. 

---

## Step 2: Identify the Type of Issue

### 1. Manufacturing Defect or Shipping Damage
Examples:
- Cracked, warped, or broken record  
- Off-center pressing (audible wow/flutter)  
- Skips or jumps (not user-caused)  
- Split or crushed sleeve  

**Resolution Options:**
- **Severe defect:**  
  - Offer customer choice:  
    - Keep item + Full Refund, OR  
    - Keep item + Replacement Sent
- **Minor/Medium defect:**  
  - Offer customer choice:  
    - Partial Refund (keep item), OR  
    - Replacement (return damaged item)

---

### 2. Wrong Item Received
Examples:
- Wrong title/artist  
- Wrong pressing (color, edition)  

**Resolution Options (customer chooses):**
- Partial Refund (keep item), OR  
- Replacement (return wrong item)

---

### 3. Missing Components
Examples:
- Missing inserts, posters, or codes  
- Incomplete box set  
- Missing bonus discs  

**Resolution Options (customer chooses):**
- Partial Refund (keep item), OR  
- Full Refund with Return Required (refund will be processed after the returned item is received and inspected), OR  
- Replacement (return incomplete item)

### 4. Buyers changed mind and product is sealed and unopened
- Refund with Return Required (refund will be processed after the returned item is received and inspected, the price of shipping on user)

## Step 3: Process Workflow

### A. Instant Refund (No Return Required)
1. Calulate the refund amount.
2. Process the refund through the payment platform.  
3. Notify the customer that the refund has been issued and will appear within 3–5 business days.  

### B. Refund with Return Required
1. Calulate the refund amount.
2. Send prepaid return label. Advise customer to pack item securely.  
3. Notify the customer that the refund will be processed after the returned item is received and inspected.
   
### C. Replacement Requests
1. Confirm customer’s shipping address.  
2. Check stock availability.  
3. If return required: Send prepaid return label. Advise customer to pack item securely.  
4. Create replacement order in system.  

---

## When to Escalate:
- Item value above $200   
- Evidence unclear or conflicting  
Escalate to **Team Lead / Manager Review**

---

## How to calculate refund
**How to calculate refund:**
- Estimate the **individual item’s share** of total cost  
- In case of defect apply the **defect percentage** to the defected item:
  - 100% - severe defect
  -  30% - medium defect
  -  15% - minor defect

## FAQ for Support Team

**Q1: Customer says “new” item has minor shelf wear.**  
Explain that “new” = unplayed, not always perfect packaging.  

**Q2: Customer missed 30-day window by 2–5 days.**  
Use judgment. If reasonable and genuine, approve and note as exception.

**Q3: Customer claims damage but item delivered 3 weeks ago.**  
Politely explain: damage must be reported within 7 days.  

**Q4: Customer wants replacement but item is out of stock.**  
Offer options:
- Wait for restock, OR  
- Full refund, OR  
- Partial refund (keep defective item)
